UNIVERSE :

The universe is the name that we use to describe the collection of all the things that exist in space.
It is made of many millions of millions of stars and planets and enormous clouds of gas
Astronomers can use telescopes to look at very distant galaxies.
The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is a nearly perfect sphere of hot plasma,
with internal convective motion that generates a magnetic field via a dynamo process.
It is by far the most important source of energy for life on Earth.
The Moon is an astronomical body orbiting Earth and is the planet's only natural satellite.
It is the fifth-largest satellite in the Solar System,
and by far the largest among planetary satellites relative to the size of the planet that it orbits.
